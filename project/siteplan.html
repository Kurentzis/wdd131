AquaGuide - Aquarium Keeping Resource

Website Name

AquaGuide is an informational resource for aquarists focused on nutrient balance in planted aquariums.

Domain: aquaguide.ru

Website Purpose

The website provides aquarists with tools and information for maintaining optimal micro- and macronutrient balance in planted aquariums.

Usage Scenarios

  • How to calculate the required amount of fertilizers for my aquarium?
  • What symptoms indicate potassium deficiency in aquarium plants?
  • What should be the ratio of nitrates to phosphates in a planted aquarium?

Website Pages

1. Fertilizer Calculator

Interactive tool for calculating fertilizer dosages based on aquarium volume and fertilizer composition.

2. Micronutrients

Detailed information about micronutrients (iron, manganese, zinc, copper, boron, etc.), their role in plant life, deficiency and excess symptoms.

3. Macronutrients

Information about macronutrients (nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, calcium, magnesium), their importance in aquariums, recommended concentrations and adjustment methods.
